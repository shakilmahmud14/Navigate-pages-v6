Cookie Policy

Last updated: 27 October 2025 — Memzo Copilot

1) Purpose of this Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Memzo Copilot (“we”, “us”, “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies on our websites and applications (“Services”), and how you can control them.

2) What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow a site to recognise your browser, store preferences, and enhance functionality. Similar technologies include local storage, pixels, and SDKs.

4) Consent Management

On your first visit, a banner asks you to accept all cookies, reject non‑essential cookies, or manage preferences by category. Your selection is stored for up to 12 months (unless you clear your browser cookies). You can change your choices anytime via Cookie Settings in the site footer.

5) Managing Cookies in Your Browser

Most browsers let you block or delete cookies. Guides:

BrowserHow to manage cookies
ChromeSettings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
SafariPreferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
EdgeSettings → Site Permissions → Cookies and site data
FirefoxSettings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

Blocking some cookies may impact certain features (e.g., persistent login).

6) Cookies Set by Third Parties

Some analytics or login providers may place their own cookies (e.g., Google Sign‑In or LinkedIn OAuth). These third parties act as independent controllers under their own policies. We review vendors to ensure appropriate privacy and security safeguards.

7) Retention and Expiry

Cookies are stored for different periods: session cookies expire when you close your browser; persistent cookies remain until deleted or reach their expiry date (typically 3–12 months).

8) Updates to this Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be notified via banner or email. The latest version will always be available on this page.
